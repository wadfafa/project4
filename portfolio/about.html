About Me

Hello I am a third year student at the University of Oregon

Walker Cullins is a dynamic digital strategist, entrepreneur, and creative enthusiast currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the University of Oregon. With an impressive background co-founding multiple ventures including Oura Digital, Atlas Analytica, and serving as a Digital Marketing Strategist for Faculty World, Walker has consistently demonstrated expertise in marketing strategy, paid advertising, and team leadership. His knack for blending analytical precision with entrepreneurial creativity has led him to successfully advise prominent direct-to-consumer brands and manage profitable advertising campaigns even within limited budgets. Beyond his entrepreneurial endeavors, Walker passionately explores the art of storytelling through the timeless medium of film photography. Often capturing moments with his cherished Olympus Pen FV, his photographic work reflects a deep appreciation for authenticity, nostalgia, and artistic expression. This blend of technical skill, creative instinct, and professional experience uniquely positions Walker as someone adept at bridging the worlds of business, technology, and visual arts, always driven by curiosity and a commitment to impactful storytelling.
